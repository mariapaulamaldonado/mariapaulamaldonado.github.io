Ruderal Dinner

A seasonal dinner with a performative twist on unwanted plants and thoughts. In collaboration with Thomas Mayer and Michelle Madsen. Make-up, Berlin, 2022.

A big table, unmatching cutlery, nettle pesto, foraged mushrooms (eatable and not), uneven chairs, a white tablecloth, and baked polenta. Another table, a typewriter, a bonfire, fingers typing words translated from thoughts (some undesired), paper, a microphone, a loudspeaker, sentences repeated out loud in loops, lights, and chairs. A dinner and an experimental ritual to deal better together with unwanted plants, unwanted thoughts, and unwanted feelings.
